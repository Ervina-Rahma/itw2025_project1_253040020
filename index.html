Project

Seorang Tuan putri yang berselancar di pegunungan salju untuk kabur dari kerajaan. sepanjang perjalanan, Ia melakukan lompatan, dan menghindari rintangan untuk mengumpulkan koin.

Happiness is the peak of ecstasy that a human can feel, especially when that happiness is born of love. Amara is a loving woman, full of compassion and affection. The man fortunate enough to receive all of it is named Kael, a considerate, caring man who cherishes Amara, the perfect partner for her. But ever since Amara’s mother, Amoria, was struck by Alzheimer’s disease, slowly being consumed until she forgot her own daughter and eventually succumbed to death, Amara’s mind has been thrown into chaos, like a storm battering the harbor of her thoughts. Will Amara be able to make the right decisions after her mother’s death? Or will she choose death itself as her new companion?
